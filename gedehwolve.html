OUR AMBITION

Our ambition is to be a ture family and community football club and to always act with honesty, integrity and respect.

Supporting and trusting young people is ambedded into the culture of Gedeh Wolve F.C

Our Mission

To Promote and foster a love of the national and international game of football for players of all age and expesially for those who live B'hai District of Grand Gedeh County and its surrounding area.

Our Vision

  1. Give every player, coach and referee the opportunity to develop their skills to the best of their ability.
  2. Provide a healthy, safe and enjoyable soccer environment for all participants.
  3. Provide a level of competition equal to each participant’s ability, interest and desire.
  4. Foster and promote sportsmanship, honesty, integrity and goodwill within the soccer community.
  5. To create a training environment that promotes hard work and self-improvement.
